OREO MILKSHAKE

Ingredients:

    for the milkshake:

  • 2 ice cubes
  • 400 ml milk
  • 12 oreo biscuits
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 scoops chocolate ice cream

    • for the garnish:

  • spraying cream
  • sauce(e.g:chocolate,toffee)

Method:

  1. First, pour your milk, sugar and vanilla into a blender and blend on a high speed for 1 minute.
  2. Next, add in your Oreo biscuits and blend until well combined.
  3. Then, add your chocolate ice cream in blend till combined
  4. Pour your milkshake in to 2 glasses and spray your cream on top, squirt a bit of your sauce on top as well.
  5. Enjoy!
